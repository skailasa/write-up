\section*{Abstract}
In this document I discuss some of the main result of measure theory. We begin by discussing algebras and $\sigma$-algebras, but move on to outer measures, specifically the Lebesgue Measure on Euclidean Space. Finally, we conclude with by applying these concepts to functions and their integration, and introduce the monotone and dominated convergence theorems.
